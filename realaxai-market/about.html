Frequently Asked Questions

Relaxor Essentials: Frequently Asked Questions

01
Does Relaxar handle tenant and operational management?

Yes. Our Unified Command Center consolidates tenant data, lease tracking, rent collection analytics, maintenance requests, and operational KPIs in one dashboard—giving you complete visibility and control.

02
How is Relaxar different from traditional property management software?

Traditional tools require manual input and generate static reports. Relaxar uses AI agents that work 24/7 to continuously analyze your data, monitor markets, flag risks, and deliver real-time, actionable insights—not just reports you have to interpret yourself.

03
What problems does Relaxar solve?

Relaxar eliminates:

• Data fragmentation across multiple systems
• Time-consuming manual analysis and reporting
• Delayed decision-making due to slow insights
• Hidden risks from incomplete information
• Compliance headaches and audit preparation stress

04
What can Relaxar's AI agents do?

Our AI agents specialize in three core areas:
• Investment Analysis Agent: Runs financial models, valuations, scenario analysis, and risk assessments in real time
• Market Intelligence Agent: Monitors market trends, comparable transactions, regulatory changes, and economic indicators
• Compliance & Legal Agent: Tracks contracts, validates compliance, flags risks, and automates audit-ready documentation

05
Can Relaxar integrate with my existing systems?

Absolutely. Relaxar seamlessly connects with your existing data sources—spreadsheets, property management systems, accounting software, CRM platforms, and cloud storage. We make integration simple with drag-and-drop uploads and API connections.
